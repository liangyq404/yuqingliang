Yuqing Liang is an artist based in New York. Her research lies in the intersection of speculative design and multi-sensorial experience. Currently pursuring her masters degree in Parsons School of Design, she has been exploring the relationship between body and the surroundings, and tries to focus on perceiving the hidden layer of human culture. ...evoking emoitons through materials and rituals. Her work involves interactive installations, audio-visuals and creative coding.
